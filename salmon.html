Baked Old Bay速 Salmon

A super easy way to cook salmon with Old Bay速 seasoning. The mayonnaise keeps the fish very tender.

  • 1 serving cooking spray
  • 1 (2 pound) salmon filet
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay速)

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
  2. Place salmon on the prepared baking sheet. Spread with mayonnaise and sprinkle with Old Bay速 seasoning.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
